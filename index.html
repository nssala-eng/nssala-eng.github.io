What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Why learn AI early?

  • AI is shaping the future of jobs
  • Early exposure builds problem-solving skills
  • Students can become creators, not just users

About NSSALA

Many Nigerian secondary schools struggle with overcrowded classrooms, limited teachers, and restricted access to digital tools.

NSSALA is a prototype that demonstrates how AI-based learning assistants can support students even in low-resource environments.

Project Status: Prototype
